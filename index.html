Welcome to Moody Kittens!

An online game to experience the joy of owning a cat!

You can begin by adding a custom kitten by entering their name at the top right. The kitten's mood is affected by petting and giving them delicious gravy. But, if your kitten's mood drops to zero, they will run away!
Good Luck and Have Fun!
